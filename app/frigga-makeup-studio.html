    Meet Komal Singh

    With over 5 years of dedicated experience in professional makeup artistry, Komal Singh has established herself as one of the most sought-after makeup artists in the region. Her journey began with a passion for enhancing natural beauty and has grown into a full-fledged studio and academy.

    Trained under industry veterans and certified by CIDESCO, Komal brings global makeup techniques to local sensibilities — creating looks that are both culturally rooted and internationally trendy.

    Her studio, Frigga Makeup Studio & Academy, is a space where clients receive bespoke beauty experiences and aspiring artists receive world-class training.
